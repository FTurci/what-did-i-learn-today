# Tap equations

The TAP equations are mean-field equations that relate the local magnetizations $m_i$ to the external fields and the couplings 

$$\tanh^{-1} m_i = h_i+\sum_j J_{ij} m_j - m_i \sum_i J^2_{ij} (1-m_j^2)$$
